Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 280 g (2 + 1/3 cups ) All Purpose Flour
  • 45 g (4 tablespoons - 1/3 cup) Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 150 g (2/3 cup) Caster Sugar
  • 8 g (2 teaspoons) Baking Soda
  • pinch of Salt
  • 20 g (1 + 1/2 tablespoons) Vinegar
  • 105 g (1/2 cup) Flavorless Vegetable Oil (Sunflower Seed Oil)
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Paste
  • 320 ml (1 + 1/3 cups) Hot Water

Chocolate Ganache:

  • 150 g (5 oz) Dark Chocolate
  • 50 g (2 oz) Milk Chocolate
  • 120 g (1/2 cup) Whipping Cream (35% fat)

Instructions:

  1. In a big bowl, sift together the dry ingredients (Flour, Cocoa, Sugar, Baking Soda, Salt) and mix until combined.
  2. In a big bowl, sift together the dry ingredients (Flour, Cocoa, Sugar, Baking Soda, Salt) and mix until combined.
  3. Pour the cake batter in a greased and lined 20 cm - 8 inch cake pan; bake in preheated oven at 180Â°C for about 40 minutes (or until a toothpick comes out clean).
  4. Remove from the oven, let cool slightly then remove the cake from the pan and cool down completely on a wire rack.
  5. Meanwhile prepare the chocolate ganache. Chop the chocolate and place it in a heat proof bowl.
  6. Place the whipping cream in a small saucepan and heat until almost starts boiling at the edges (don't bring it to a full boil).
  7. Pour the hot whipping cream onto the chopped chocolate, let sit for 1-2 minutes then stir until melted and smooth. If the ganache is too runny, you can wait for few minutes before place it on top of the cake.
  8. Place the ganache on top of the cake and decorate with a spoon to create whirls or flower shape.
  9. Let the ganache set a bit at room temperature before serving.
  10. Enjoy :))
