27.09.2019

This strike was part of the “Week For Future”. As its name implies, throughout this week (from the 20th to the 26th of September) we developed different activities to mobilize protesters for the 27th.
This demonstration was not only open to young people but everyone. The national nucleus joined with several organizations and collectives to expand the protest to civil society and unions, trying to involve students, workers, and other citizens who wanted to join. A list of supporters and subscribers, “Save the Climate”, was created at a national level.
